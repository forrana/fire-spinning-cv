Alexey Kuchyn

Passionate fire speaner who fall in love with a fire art after the very first fire performance watched.

Have never seen the fire art as the way to earn money or popularity. The only reason I do it - I really like it. I like the sound, the smell, the hotness, the feeling of it. It helps me to feel alive. I admire flame, and trying to be a part of it while perform.

I like to spin it alone, with friends for public. My motto is 'Give me kerosene and show the stage'. Always trying to master my existing tricks and learn new ones. Particularry interested in a heavy equipment.
